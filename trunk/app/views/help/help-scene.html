Controls:
  1. Two Different Control Options:
    • Tilt:
      Tilt you phone to move the skier, right and left to go right and left and up and down to increase your speed (Note: Small movements are best).
    • Keyboard:
      Use the 'a' and 'd' keys to move the skier right or left, and tilt up or down to increase or decrease your speed.
  2. You can use the 'f' key (or On-Screen Faster Button) for a temporary speed boost. The 'f' key affect lasts as long as you are holding it, the On-Screen Faster button lasts for 2 seconds.
  3. Hit ramps to avoid obstacles and increase your score.
