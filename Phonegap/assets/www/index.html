      Help
      1. Controls:
        • Touch:
          1. Touch the left side of the screen to move left, right side to move right.
          2. Tilt the device forward or back to increase/decrease your speed.
      2. You can shake the device for a temporary speed boost (could be helpful in sticky situations).
      3. Hit ramps to avoid obstacles and increase your score.
      4. If you see an * next to your score on the local High Score list, then that score was not submitted to the global High Score list (usually means there was a network error of some sort).
